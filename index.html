Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

We specialize in premium varieties including Shiitake, Oyster (Pearl, Blue, and Pink), Lion's Mane, and Reishi mushrooms. Each variety is cultivated using organic methods to ensure maximum nutritional value and flavor.

We maintain strict quality control through every step of cultivation, harvesting, and processing. Our mushrooms are tested for purity, nutritional content, and safety. We hold international quality certifications and follow Good Agricultural Practices (GAP).

Yes, we ship to multiple countries across Africa, Europe, and the Middle East. We use specialized packaging to maintain freshness during transit. Contact us for specific shipping rates and delivery times to your location.

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. For fresh mushrooms, our minimum is 5kg. For processed products like powders and tablets, minimum orders start from 100 units. We can accommodate both small and large orders.

Fresh mushrooms should be stored in a paper bag in the refrigerator (not plastic). They'll stay fresh for 5-7 days. Avoid washing before storage - clean just before use. For longer storage, consider our dried or powdered mushroom products.
