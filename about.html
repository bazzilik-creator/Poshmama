Our Story

Growing premium organic mushrooms with passion and purpose since 2024

Welcome to Posh Mama Farms

Founded in 2024, Posh Mama Farms began as a small family operation with a big dream: to provide the highest quality organic mushrooms while practicing sustainable agriculture. What started as a passion project has grown into a thriving farm that serves health-conscious customers across the region.

Company Summary

Posh mama is a start up women owned company based and registered in Kampala Uganda and will majorly be involved in the growing, drying, powdering a range of high quality mushrooms for both local, regional and international markets.

The company has developed a product mix which will committed to harnessing the full potential of mushrooms for both culinary, therapeutical and medicinal use, meeting the demands of global markets

With a strong focus on sustainable farming practices, and a commitment to innovation, Posh Mama aims to promote the health benefits of mushrooms while supporting local agricultural communities and preserving natural ecosystems.

The team includes experienced, result oriented Agricultural and food science individuals from production, processing, food and export sectors.

The company is a start up and looks at strengthening itself with likeminded partners.
