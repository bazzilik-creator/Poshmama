Our Story

Where Empowered hands meets Global perfection

Posh Mama Farms

Posh Mama Farms is a forward-thinking women owned agribusiness company based and registered in Kampala Uganda dedicated to empowering farmers, processors, and exporters with sustainable, high-impact solutions. We specialize in exports, certifications, sustainable sourcing, packaging, processing plant design, and quality management systems. Our expert team supports clients with business plans, grant applications, shipment organization, and export readiness, integrating smart technologies that boost efficiency and global competitiveness. At Posh Mama Farms, we grow trust, sustain futures, and help agribusinesses thrive responsibly in international markets.

At Posh Mama Farms, we are redefining sustainable agribusiness in Africa — empowering communities, protecting the planet, and delivering world-class agricultural excellence. As both a premium producer and trusted agribusiness, we work hand in hand with farmers, cooperatives, and enterprises to promote ethical sourcing, sustainable certifications, food safety, and smart agricultural solutions. From cultivating high-quality produce to guiding partners in achieving global standards, our mission is to transform agriculture into a force for prosperity and environmental stewardship. Every harvest, every partnership, and every innovation at Posh Mama Farms is dedicated to nurturing people, planet, and premium quality.

Our services

1. Consultancy

At Posh Mama Farms, we deliver smart, end-to-end agribusiness consultancy that drives compliance, quality, and sustainable growth. From certifications in food safety (HACCP, BRC, ISO 22000, GMP, KOSHER, IFS), organic (EU, NOP, JAS, ROC), biodynamic, GlobalG.A.P., social standards (SMETA, FFL) ISO 14001, ISO 9001, CTPAT and ethical sourcing, to packaging, processing plant design, agro-processing equipment, and quality management systems, we cover every step. We also support business plans, grant applications, site inspections, shipment logistics, smart technology integration, and seamless access to international markets—helping agribusinesses operate responsibly, efficiently, and competitively on a global scale.

2. Specialised in sourcing of the following products both in organic and non- organic certified qualities from ethical sourcing. We work with sustainable partners in different parts of the world to support your value chain in ensuring quality and reliable supply from production to shipping at your door.
