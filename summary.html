Company Overview

About Posh Mama Farms

Posh Mama Farms is a pioneering women-owned startup company based and registered in Kampala, Uganda. We are majorly involved in the growing, drying, and powdering of a comprehensive range of high-quality mushrooms for local, regional, and international markets.

Our Product Excellency

The company has developed an innovative product mix committed to harnessing the full potential of mushrooms for culinary, therapeutic, and medicinal applications. We meet the demanding requirements of global markets through our unwavering focus on quality and consistency.
